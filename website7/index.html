Introduction

This website was created with the aim to direct Boston College students and people in general to helpful resources regarding mental health.

Similar to when someone goes to the doctor for medical aid, anyone suffering with mental health should reach out for help.

Below are some key factors that can lead to mental health issues:

  • stress & anxiety
  • confidence - self-belief, self-image, courage
  • pressures of life - social media, work load, relationships, goals, expectations
  • coping with set-backs, criticism, persistence

If you are experiencing any of the above factors be sure to look around the rest of the website for tips and follow the provided links for more information

Resilience is...

  • the capacity to recover quickly from difficulties
  • the ability to spring back
  • an ongoing learning process

Resilience is NOT...

  • an absence of adversity
  • always obvious
  • complicated
