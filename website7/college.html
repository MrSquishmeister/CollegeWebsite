Boston College

Boston College have a designated team to help with any concerns you might have about your own or someone else's safety in regards to bullying, harassment, abuse or neglect.

If you would like to speak to a member of the 'Safeguarding Team', please call 01205 365701 and ask for 'Safeguarding'

Learner Support Officers can work with you on any difficulties and issues which are preventing you from achieving your goals and finding a positive way forward.

Offering a safe and confidential service, the College's Counsellors are available for you and staff to talk about any personal issues you may have.
